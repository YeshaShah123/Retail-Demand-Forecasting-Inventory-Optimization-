# Business Insights Section

Use these as dashboard text boxes or talking points during a portfolio walkthrough.

- West generated the highest revenue at approximately `$3.60M`, followed by Ontario at approximately `$3.06M`.
- Technology was the strongest revenue category at approximately `$5.98M`, making it the clearest demand growth focus area.
- The inventory simulation identified roughly `29%` of SKU-region records as stockout risk, giving the operations team a clear replenishment queue.
- Roughly `26%` of SKU-region records are flagged as overstock, suggesting working capital is tied up in slow or excess inventory.
- Restock recommendations should be prioritized by high monthly demand, low inventory level, and longer supplier lead time.
- Seasonal demand should be monitored at category-region level because regional buying patterns can hide inside national totals.
- Forecasting is intentionally simple and explainable: a moving average model is enough to support weekly or monthly operations planning.

## Stakeholder Framing

For a Customer Operations Analyst:

- Which regions are creating demand spikes?
- Which SKUs are likely to disappoint customers due to low stock?
- Which products need replenishment follow-up?

For a Business Analyst:

- Which categories drive revenue and margin?
- Where are inventory policies misaligned with demand?
- What operational actions should be taken this month?

For a Sales or Revenue Operations role:

- Which segments and regions are growing?
- Which products are top sellers but operationally constrained?
- How does inventory availability affect revenue visibility?
